How to Play

01
Fill every row with digits 1–9. No digit may repeat in a row.
02
Fill every column with digits 1–9. No digit may repeat in a column.
03
Fill every 3×3 box with digits 1–9. No digit may repeat in a box.
04
Use logic alone to deduce each number. No guessing required.
05
Notes Mode lets you pencil in candidates — tap N or the Notes button.
06
Every mistake costs −100 pts. Every hint costs −150 pts. Solve fast for a bonus!
